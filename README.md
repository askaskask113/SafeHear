SafeHear is a smart listening safety prototype designed to protect users from hearing damage caused by prolonged exposure to high audio volumes.

## Problem Statement
Excessive headphone usage at unsafe volume levels can lead to hearing fatigue and long-term auditory damage, especially among students and professionals.

## Solution
SafeHear continuously monitors audio volume and listening duration, detects unsafe patterns, alerts users in real time, and automatically adjusts volume to safe levels.

## Key Features
- Real-time volume monitoring
- Unsafe volume detection & alerts
- Auto volume control
- Smart listening timers (study, music, sleep)
- Relaxation exercises to reduce ear fatigue
- AI assistant for hearing safety guidance
